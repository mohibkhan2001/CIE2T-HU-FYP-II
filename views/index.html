About CIE²T

The Cambridge International Education system encounters challenges like manual workflows and communication gaps. To address this, a proposed web application aims to automate question management, exam paper generation, and assessment reporting. The goal is to enhance efficiency and transparency within the Cambridge framework, benefiting students, educators, and institutions.
